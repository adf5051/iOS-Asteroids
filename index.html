Simple space physics like drift and acceleration. The player can also screen wrap and shoot asteroids like the original game.

There will be a simple instruction screen for showing the player how to play

The player will need to learn the physics and sliding while shooting and dodging the asteroids. As time goes on the asteroids get faster so the player will have opportunity to imporve.
